Message from Leadership

Zohair Naseer

Chief Executive Officer & Chairman

"We do not build companies for quarters.
We build institutions for generations."

I started in this industry when a "trade exhibition" in Pakistan meant a tent, a generator, and a lot of hoping for the best. Twenty years and 1,500 events later, the tents are gone but the standard hasn't changed: if a client puts their name on an event we're running, we treat it like our own reputation is on the line - because it is.

We turn down work that doesn't fit what we can deliver well. We'd rather be the team a government body or a chamber of commerce calls back for the tenth time than the one that wins a pitch once. That's the only kind of growth I've ever cared about building here.
