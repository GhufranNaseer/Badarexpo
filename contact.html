FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about space booking, custom stall fabrication, and visitor access.

How do I book an exhibition space or custom stall for upcoming events?

You can book spaces by selecting your preferred event on our calendar list and clicking the inquiry button, or by filling out the form at the bottom of this page. Our project coordinators will get in touch to assist you with floor layout availability, pricing, and shell schemes.

How do I become an event sponsor?

Badar Expo Solutions offers tiered sponsorship slots (Platinum, Gold, Silver, and Category sponsorships). Reach out directly to our Sales team via the corporate form or at sales@badarexpo.com to receive our comprehensive sponsorship catalog and branding matrices.

How can I participate as a visitor or B2B buyer?

Visitor registration links go live on our website 30 days before each major trade show. You can register online to receive your electronic entry badge, or check in directly at the visitor registration desks on show days.

Can international companies join and get custom support?

Absolutely. We provide dedicated support for foreign exhibitors, including customs clearance coordination for machinery displays, temporary visa invitation letters, translation services, hotel reservations, and pre-scheduled B2B buyer meetings.

How long before an event should I register to book a custom stall?

For custom stall design and structural fabrication, we recommend initiating designs 60 to 90 days before the show. This ensures adequate time for structural approvals, engineering reviews, material sourcing, and mock fabrication in our workshops.
