What We Actually Do

Not a vendor list. A single build process.

Ask five agencies for "services" and you'll get five separate quotes that don't talk to each other. We run all three disciplines under one roof, so the stand design, the AV rig, and the buyer outreach are planned together from day one - not stitched together the week before doors open.

Plan & Manage

Venue, vendors, timelines, and visitor flow - exhibitions, conferences, and corporate events run end to end by one project team.

Build & Produce

Stalls, canopy structures, LED walls, AV rigs, furniture, and signage - designed, fabricated, and installed in-house.

Market & Grow

B2B matchmaking, PR, and digital production that put buyers, exhibitors, and delegates in the room on the day.

How We Work

Six stages. One project lead, start to finish.

The same team that scopes the brief signs off on the build, so nothing gets re-explained halfway through.

01

Consultation

We learn the objective - buyers, launch, or brand platform - before we talk logistics.

02

Planning

Venue, budget, timeline, and vendor scope locked into a single project plan.

03

Design

Stall, stage, and platform concepts developed alongside the marketing plan, not after it.

04

Execution

In-house fabrication, installation, and rehearsal, managed on site by our own crew.

05

Delivery

Doors open. On-ground coordination keeps exhibitors, delegates, and press on schedule.

06

Support

Teardown, reporting, and a debrief on what to carry into the next platform.
