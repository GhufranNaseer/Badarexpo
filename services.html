FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I organize an event with Badar Expo Solutions?

Simply reach out through our contact form or hotline. A dedicated project manager will schedule a consultation, understand your objectives, and prepare a proposal covering scope, timeline, and budget.

Can you manage international exhibitions?

Yes. We regularly coordinate international delegations, government pavilions, and cross-border exhibitor logistics for global exhibitions and trade fairs.

Do you provide stall design and fabrication in-house?

Yes, our in-house fabrication team handles custom builds, Octanorm shell schemes, LED integration, and furniture rental — so quality and timelines stay under our control.

Can you manage complete conferences and seminars?

Absolutely. We handle speaker coordination, AV production, delegate registration, and on-ground execution for conferences of any scale.

How can I become an exhibitor at your events?

Visit our Events page to view upcoming exhibitions, or contact our team directly for booth availability, pricing, and participation packages.
