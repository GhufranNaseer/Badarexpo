Common Inquiries

Answers to general questions about B2B profiling, setup requirements, and ad run lengths.

How is buyer data qualified for B2B Matchmaking?

All visiting buyers submit business registration documents, purchase-capacity figures, and procurement priorities. Our team manually reviews and screens these submissions to ensure they represent active buying intent before scheduling sessions.

What ad spend levels are expected for Event Marketing campaigns?

Ad run costs are determined based on your target audience size, sector, and registration length. We manage ad deployment within your approved corporate budgets, optimizing for high conversion rates.

Do you support hybrid and remote live-streaming services?

Yes. Our Digital Production division sets up multi-camera hardware arrays on-site to stream directly to YouTube, LinkedIn, or customized private web portals with real-time lower thirds and motion banners.
