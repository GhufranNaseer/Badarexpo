Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about space booking, custom stall fabrication, and visitor access.

How do I book an exhibition space or custom stall for upcoming events?

You can book spaces by selecting your preferred event on our calendar list and clicking the inquiry button, or by filling out the form at the bottom of this page. Our project coordinators will get in touch to assist you with floor layout availability, pricing, and shell schemes.

Do you support custom stall fabrication and booth design?

Yes, Badar Expo Solutions houses a fully integrated, award-winning stall design and fabrication division. We design custom-built wooden booths, modular double-decker pavilions, and specialized display structures matching international standards, ensuring your brand stands out on the show floor.

What is the difference between Co-Organized and Managed events?

Co-Organized events are joint ventures where BXSS shares ownership and strategic planning with chambers of commerce, government bodies, or associations. Managed events are fully client-funded corporate or government sessions (convocations, award shows, delegation visits) where BXSS acts as the exclusive execution and operations agency.

Can international exhibitors participate and get custom support?

Absolutely. We provide dedicated support for foreign exhibitors, including customs clearance coordination for machinery displays, temporary visa invitation letters, translation services, hotel reservations, and pre-scheduled B2B buyer meetings.
