12 FAQs

Construction & Fabrication FAQs

Common questions about how we design, build and install exhibition structures.

01Do you fabricate stalls in-house or outsource?

Everything is built at our own workshop - steel, wood, printing and electrical are all in-house, so quality, timelines and cost stay under our direct control.

02How long does a custom stall take to build?

Depending on size and complexity, custom builds typically take 2–4 weeks from CAD sign-off to installation, with standard Octanorm builds turning around faster.

03Can you handle installation for international exhibitions?

Yes, our teams coordinate transport, customs and on-site installation crews for exhibitions outside Pakistan, working with local partners where needed.

04What safety standards do your fabrication crews follow?

All crews follow hard hat, electrical and fire safety protocols, with every structure passing a quality inspection before it is cleared for the exhibition floor.

05Do you provide 3D visualization before construction begins?

Yes, every project moves through CAD drawing and photoreal 3D visualization for client approval before any material is cut in the workshop.
